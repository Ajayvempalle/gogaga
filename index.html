Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a state in northern India. It covers 342,239 square kilometres or 10.4 per cent of India's total geographical area. It is the largest Indian state by area and the seventh largest by population

Andaman

Andaman and Nicobar Islands are developing into a major tourism hub with exotic-looking beaches and pristine islands having equally exotic names, wonderful opportunities for adventure sports like snorkelling and sea-walking etc.

Coorg

Nestled in the folds of the majestic Western Ghats, Coorg offers vast stretches of coffee plantations, beautiful flowers and stunning vistas to boot. No wonder it is one of the most popular destinations in southern India. The hill station entices tourists with its tropical weather all through the year.

Kerala

Named as one of the ten paradises of the world by National Geographic Traveler, Kerala is famous especially for its ecotourism initiatives and beautiful backwaters.

Himachal

This is popularly renowned for its Himalayan landscapes and popular hill-stations. Many outdoor activities such as rock climbing, mountain biking, paragliding, ice-skating, trekking, rafting, and heli-skiing are popular tourist attractions in Himachal Pradesh.

Kashmir

Snowcapped Himalayas, fascinating lakes, significant pilgrimage sites and awe-inspiring pastures form the major attractions of Jammu and Kashmir, one of the most popular tourist destinations in India.

Hotel Booking

Single Room

Single room: these rooms are assigned to one person or a couple. It may have one or more beds, but the size of the bed depends on the hotel.

Double Room

What is a Double room in a hotel? A double room is a hotel room with a double bed that can accommodate two people. Double rooms come in different sizes, and the room size can affect the room rate. A double room can be a good option for couples or solo travelers who want more space than a single room.

Suit Room

Suites are usually considered to be the largest style of hotel room available in most hotels. Suites usually offer multiple separated spaces: Bathroom, kitchen, bedroom, living area, and sometimes many more.
